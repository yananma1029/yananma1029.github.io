Yanan Ma

I am a PhD candidate in the Natural Language Processing and Text Mining group at the University of Manchester (expected to graduate in March 2025), under the supervision of Prof. Goran Nenadic, Dr. Sabine van der Veer, and Dr. Lamiece Hassan. Before my PhD, I received my master degree at South China Normal University, and worked 3 years at the information department of a hospital. I received the University of Manchester - China Scholarship Council joint scholarship, and Turning Scheme (2025) funding during my PhD study. I previous won the Outstanding Graduate Joint Training Program Scholarship, South China Normal University in 2018.

Research

My research interests lie in natural language processing, large language model, text mining, and health AI. Specifically, I recently focus on large language model finetuning, benchmarking, alignment and reasoning, with a particular interest in the biomedical domain.
